# SECR & PPN 06/21 Report Generation - Compliance Analysis

## Overview
This document compares our generated reports against the official UK Government requirements for SECR and PPN 06/21 compliance.

## SECR (Streamlined Energy and Carbon Reporting) Compliance

### ✅ **Required Elements - Our Implementation**

#### **1. Company Information**
- ✅ Company name
- ✅ Company number (optional)
- ✅ Reporting period
- ✅ Director's name and title
- ✅ Reporting date

#### **2. Emissions Data**
- ✅ **Scope 1 Emissions**: Direct emissions from owned/controlled sources
- ✅ **Scope 2 Emissions**: Indirect emissions from purchased energy
- ✅ **Scope 3 Emissions**: Other indirect emissions
- ✅ **Total Emissions**: Sum of all scopes
- ✅ **Emissions Intensity Ratio**: tCO₂e per £1M turnover

#### **3. Energy Consumption**
- ✅ Total energy consumption (kWh)
- ✅ Year-on-year comparison (if previous year data available)
- ✅ Energy reduction percentage

#### **4. Year-on-Year Performance**
- ✅ Previous year emissions comparison
- ✅ Reduction/increase percentage calculation
- ✅ Performance trend analysis

#### **5. Energy Efficiency Measures**
- ✅ Description of implemented measures
- ✅ Automated carbon tracking system
- ✅ Energy consumption monitoring
- ✅ Energy-saving initiatives

#### **6. Methodology**
- ✅ UK Government GHG Conversion Factors (2024)
- ✅ Carbonmate automated calculation system
- ✅ Compliance with official standards

#### **7. Director's Statement**
- ✅ Confirmation of accuracy and completeness
- ✅ Director signature and title
- ✅ Company name and date

### **📋 SECR Report Structure**
1. **Title Page** - "Streamlined Energy and Carbon Reporting (SECR)"
2. **Executive Summary** - SECR requirements explanation
3. **Emissions Summary Table** - Scope 1/2/3 breakdown
4. **Energy Consumption** - Total consumption data
5. **Emissions Intensity** - tCO₂e per £1M turnover
6. **Year-on-Year Comparison** - Performance tracking
7. **Energy Efficiency Measures** - Implemented initiatives
8. **Methodology** - Calculation methods and factors
9. **Director's Statement** - Official sign-off
